International Driving License (Permit)

Practical assistance for travelers who need an International Driving Permit for overseas driving.

Drive abroad with the right permit in place

Planning to drive abroad? Driving legally in many countries requires an International Driving Permit (IDP), whether you're travelling across Europe by car or working in the US. An international driving licence in India allows you to rent and drive vehicles overseas with ease.

If you’re wondering how to obtain one, RVX Travels can guide and help you obtain the IDP. Your one-stop solution in managing the complete end to end application process including, required documents, eligibility, and associated fees.

  • Support for common travel-related IDP questions
  • Quick enquiry assistance for timelines and processing
  • Useful for leisure, family visit, and multi-country road travel plans
